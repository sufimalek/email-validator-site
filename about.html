About BounceFree

We're on a mission to solve email deliverability challenges and help businesses maintain clean, effective email lists that drive real results.

Our Story

BounceFree was born from a simple observation: too many businesses were struggling with email deliverability issues that were hurting their bottom line. Bounce rates were high, sender reputations were damaged, and marketing campaigns were failing.

Founded in 2020 by a team of email marketing veterans and software engineers, we set out to create a solution that would make email validation accessible, accurate, and easy to use for businesses of all sizes.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 businesses validate more than 2.5 million emails daily, reducing bounce rates by an average of 30% and significantly improving deliverability rates.
